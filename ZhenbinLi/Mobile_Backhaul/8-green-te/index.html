Green TE

1. OSPF Extensions for MPLS Green Traffic Engineering

Publication: IETF Individual Draft

Publication History: 2013-02-18

Publication URL: https://tools.ietf.org/html/draft-li-ospf-ext-green-te-00

Description:

The energy-saving is one important topic in the world, and now most of technology for energy-saving is related with the hardware design instead of that based on the whole network. This document proposes OSPF extensions to synchronize the energy-saving parameter of each node in the network which will be considered when the LSP path is calculated.
